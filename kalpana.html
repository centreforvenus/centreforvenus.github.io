Ar. Kalpana Raghavendra heads the Architecture and Urban Planning practice at VENUS.

Mrs Kalpana specialises in Architecture, Design, Interior Decoration, and Urban Planning. She specialises in sustainable habitat design, green building principles, and in a vernacular idiom of expression. She has nearly a decade and a half of experience in shelter planning and interior design of residences and commercial office space.

Mrs Kalpana blends theoretical principles of design with use of sustainable material and visually appealing, uniquely identifiable form that follows function.

She has been teaching in architecture and Urban planning institutes from over 20 years.

She was instrumental in developing “Teaching Learning Methodology “for various schools of Architecture.
