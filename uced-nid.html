Centre for VENUS commenced Coaching and Training for various B.Des Programs in the year 2018. The Centre developed and delivered training for various B.Des Entrance Exams, i.e. UCEED, NID, NIFT by developing core design development skills in all our enrolled students.

Our Program will foster design thinking and aptitude for creative approaches to problem solving. We use and deploy learning methods aimed at harnessing the most able of talent in students aiming to pursue design as a career.
