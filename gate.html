We commenced offer of Gate AR Coaching at the Center for VENUS in the year 2016. The Program is designed and developed at holistic improvement of Knowledge and Skills of both B.Arch and B.Plan students.

Our teaching methodology comprises group discussions, lectures, analysis of current issues, high impact contribution in design and architecture at Local, National, and Global Levels.

Our team of Expert Faculty from various disciplines with notable professional and academic contributions and with impeccable reputation contribute at the Centre. We enabler 3600 development of our students into successful architects and urban planners.
