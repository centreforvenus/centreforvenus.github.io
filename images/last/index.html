India will continue to be at the forefront of global growth and development.

At the heart of India’s growth narrative is the nation’s cities, our common, shared future.

The Centre for VENUS (Vibrant, Efficient, Networked Urban Settlements) was founded in 2015 to make a positive, lasting impact on our cities, both in the present and future.

We render our contribution through research, consultancy, teaching, training, advocacy, and change management.

Our contribution straddles the disciplines of design, architecture, urban planning, local and regional futures visioning, and urban policy.

For You Here !

Cities hold promise to us all but the promise is largely undelivered on many parameters such as walkability, clean air, public transport, water, green spaces, livelihoods, governance, and much else.

Centre for VENUS endeavours to deliver this wholesome promise of cities to everyday citizens, including pedestrians and the upwardly mobile.

We at VENUS believe our cities must transform from ville spontanée (spontaneous city) to ville créée (designed city) to deliver for example improved urban experience to all.

We also may decouple development from its negative externalities.

VENUS envisions transformation of lives through our contribution in the built form, city planning, and policy development.

Transformation is our way of life.

For You Here !

The colours and textures of our weave, the material form and expression of architecture in our stepped wells, urban planning and design in our temple towns, resource conservation through water harvesting are only a few examples of our expression through design.

Our endeavor to mimic the west in our expression of design has resulted in chaotic cities and incoherent built form. VENUS offers its expertise in design of built form, low income housing, residential and institutional architecture, and in urban planning and design.
